What Is Jailbreak?

In this gamemode, the red team takes orders from the blue team.
If the red team does not follow these orders exactly they will be shot and killed.
blue team are referred to as "CTs", red team are referred to as "Ts".
The ratio is 1 to 3.

    General Rules

  1. There is no warden on this server, any CT can give orders. If a CT is already giving orders, do not interrupt them or you will risk being muted/teamswitched.
  2. No detours/delays is implied with every command. If there are 2 of the order referenced (such as yellow lines), it is delaying to not go to the closest.
  3. Do not ghost. (as a dead player, reveal alive players locations).
  4. AFK freeze means: do not touch your keyboard or mouse. You are allowed to type into chat, use voice commands, your microphone, and heal with your medigun, but not switching between healing and not healing.
  5. Freeze means: do not jump, switch between crouching/standing (doing it once before freezing is ok), move in any direction, use or switch between your weapons.
  6. Cell doors are opened automatically after 50 seconds on most maps, if no orders are given, it's a freeday. You may give orders over freedays.
  7. Do not turn on friendly fire except while being a CT and during Hunger Games / Arena or risk being banned.
  8. Orders are given considering a default TF2 configuration, including binds.
  9. First reaction / last reaction â€“ If a first reaction action is said, the first T to do it dies. If a last reaction action is said, the last T to do it dies.

Last Request (LR) rules

  1. The last T to follow orders (doing what everyone else has/had to do) gets a "last request", meaning they can tell CTs what to do.
  2. The last request should last 90 seconds.
  3. After the process is complete, they must kill themselves, or be killed by the CTs.
  4. If the T attacks a CT without a reason, CTs may kill them.
  5. Ts may not request anything that results in a freeday, hunger games, hat days, joke days, stupid shit such as making everyone type a command into console, modifying the way CTs give orders (unless they feel like it), or admins to use their commands (unless they feel like it).

CT Rules

  1. You must always be willing to give orders (with a good enough microphone), this gamemode isn't only about killing people.
  2. No baiting, that means if you're in melee range with a T, they are allowed to kill you without being killed themselves. If a T is charging at you, that is rebelling and you may kill them.
  3. When a T asks for a repeat, and you haven't already given one, you must comply. Do not give more than one, and do not give one if nobody asked for one.
  4. Do not ask for repeats.
  5. No armoury/vent camping.
  6. No special treatment, this means allowing a specific prisoner to do anything else besides following main orders.
  7. Opinionated games are fine, but don't make it unfair, like "guess the number I'm thinking" or "what's my favourite food".
  8. When playing Simon Says, you must state who is Simon and when Simon wants to play Simon says. You do not have to rename yourself.
  9. You may rush Ts that are delaying in the obstacle course or deathrun. If they refuse to move after 10 seconds you may shoot them. The last T to survive may run to the end without dying, as it would be a freekill otherwise.
  10. Some games are not timed, therefore you must give a countdown.
  11. During minigames like keep or pass, you are not allowed to kill more than 3 Ts at a time.
  12. During games like obstacle course, deathrun, etc., the last T standing has to make it as far as every other T has, in order to get Last Request.
  13. Do not play the same game/minigame twice in a row.
  14. As a CT, you may not kill yourself during Last Request.
  15. If you are playing Keep or Pass, you must type the prize to team chat prior to the T responding whether or not they want to keep or pass it.
